README Dedeurwaerder et al 2010
----------------------------------------------------

Clinical information
----------------------------

samplename: Sample name

gefilename: Sample name for gene expression data (Affymetrix)

geqc: Gene expression quality control (0: failed QC criteria, 1: passed QC criteria)

Infinium Sentrix Barcode: Slide reference for methylation data (Illumina)

Infinium Sample Section: Sample position on the Infinium slide

methylqc: Methylation quality control (0: failed QC criteria, 1: passed QC criteria)

"subtypeIHC: Breast cancer subtype based on immunohistochemistry/FISH data and histological grade [“basal-like”:ER-, HER2- and HG3,""HER2” : HER 3+ by IHC or amplification of HER2, “luminal A”: ER+, HER2- and histological grade 1, and “luminal B”:ER+, HER2- and histological grade 3]"

grade: Histological grade

sizebin: Tumor size (0: <=2 cm, 1: >2cm)

node: Nodal status (0:no axillary lymph node metastasis, 1: lymph node metastasis)

agebin: Age (0: < or= 50 years, 1: >50 years old)

er: ER status by immunohistochemistry (0:negative, 1:positive)

her2: HER2 status by immunohistochemistry or FISH (0:negative, 1:positive)

SubtypeGE: Breast cancer subtype based on gene expression data (cfr methods)

e.rfs: Relapse information (0: censoring, 1: relapse)

t.rfs: Relapse free survival in years

e.os: Survival information (0: censoring, 1: dead)

t.os: Overall survival time in years

Mainclusters: Pertaining to the 2 main clusters (I and II)

Clusters: Pertaining to the 6 clusters (1 to 6)

ge_LumProgenitorLIM: Luminal Progenitor signature from Lim et al. (Nat Med 2009) at the gene expression level

ge_LumMatureLIM: Luminal Mature signature from Lim et al. (Nat Med 2009) at the gene expression level

ge_StromaLIM: Stroma signature from Lim et al. (Nat Med 2009) at the gene expression level

ge_MaSCLIM: Mammary Stem Cell signature from Lim et al. (Nat Med 2009) at the gene expression level

ge_ESR1: ESR1 module from Desmedt et al. (Clin Cancer Res 2008) at the gene expression level

methyl_LumProgenitorLIM: Luminal Progenitor signature from Lim et al. (Nat Med 2009) at the methylation level

methyl_LumMatureLIM: Luminal Mature signature from Lim et al. (Nat Med 2009) at the methylation level

methyl_StromaLIM: Stroma signature from Lim et al. (Nat Med 2009) at the methylation level

methyl_MaSCLIM: Mammary Stem cell signature from Lim et al. (Nat Med 2009) at the methylation level

methyl_ESR1: ESR1 module from Desmedt et al. (Clin Cancer Res 2008) at the methylation level
